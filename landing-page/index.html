Project Description:

B1 Assignment: Usability Testing & Landing Page
Usability is a crucial step in the process of designing interfaces that are easy and intuitive to use. Without direct feedback from users my design efforts and iterations are merely guesswork. This month, I will design a series of interfaces intended for specific use. With the help of usability testing, I will see my interfaces through the eyes of test participants. More specifically, the usability tests created this month will reveal where users click when navigating my interfaces, while trying to accomplish a task or goal. The tasks (interactions) I design for users will reflect the business and user goals defined early in the "Discovery" phase of product development.
Next, I will leverage my existing knowledge of HTML5 CSS3, and GIT to build and deploy a working landing page on the gh-pages branch of your GIThub account. This landing page will act as a portal to the usability tests, and test results, I create this month.
